About Ashish Saccos

Our Mission

To encourage members to save, take loans, and actively participate in the cooperative for mutual growth and financial stability.

What We Propose

  • Encourage members to save regularly and responsibly.
  • Provide accessible loans to meet the financial needs of our members.
  • Promote community and cooperation among all members.
  • Foster a sense of financial discipline and empowerment.

Our Services

Savings Accounts

Save with us and enjoy the benefits of earning interest while keeping your money secure.

Loan Facilities

We offer flexible loan options to help you meet your financial needs. Apply now!

Financial Advice

Our experienced team offers advice on how to save, invest, and manage your finances effectively.
